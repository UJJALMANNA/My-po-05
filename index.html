About Me

I'm Ujjal Manna — currently pursuing my M.Tech in Control & Instrumentation at IIT Bhilai, after two consecutive GATE Computer Science qualifications brought me here from my B.Tech at BBIT, Kolkata.

My undergraduate years were spent building a foundation in computer science fundamentals, which I carried into GATE preparation — qualifying in both the 2025 and 2026 attempts before choosing to specialise further at IIT Bhilai. Control & Instrumentation sits at an interesting intersection: feedback loops, signal processing, and systems thinking, applied with the same rigor I'd bring to any algorithm.

Outside of coursework, I spend time helping juniors navigate their own GATE preparation, and I track what's moving in tech — both to stay sharp and because explaining a concept well is usually the fastest way to actually understand it.
